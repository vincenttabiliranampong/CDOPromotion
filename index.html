Welcome to Cagayan de Oro

City of Golden Friendship

About the City

Cagayan de Oro is a highly urbanized city in Northern Mindanao known for its vibrant economy, friendly people, and exciting outdoor adventures such as whitewater rafting.

Tourist Spots

Whitewater Rafting

Experience thrilling river adventures along the Cagayan River.

Mapawa Nature Park

A nature park perfect for hiking, camping, and outdoor activities.

Macahambus Cave

Offers a blend of nature, extreme adventure, and leisure with panoramic views of the city and Macajalar Bay..

Local Government

The city government is led by the Mayor and local officials who oversee development, public services, and community welfare.
